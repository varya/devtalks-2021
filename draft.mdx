## Costs and savings

* Component is made only once
* Component needs to be integrated

* We support only one component


## Costs formula, per component

### How much a component costs
```
$ = production time
    + (support costs * component lifetime)
    + (integration time * amount of projects)
```

### Without design system
```
$ = (
    production time + (support costs * component lifetime)
  ) * amount of projects
```

## Why to measure?

* Design system needs to prove its business value to the upper levels. If
the value is measurable, it can result into better management of resources.
* Design system team needs confirmation if they are doing the right thing.
* Design system team needs to correct its plans according to instant measurements
and dynamic picture. 

## Valuable for different levels

* (B) Measuring business value
  What is important for the business levels to know
* (P) Measuring system-project interactions
  How good the design system is for its users
* (I) Internal measuring
  Workload, internal costs, etc
